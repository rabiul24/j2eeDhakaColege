Library of Dhaka College

The Library of Dhaka College was established in 1841 at the time of establishment of the College. There are 30,000 (Thirty Thousand) books in the library with well decorated reading room.

The library provides reading support to two hundred students everyday. It opens at 8 am and closes at 4 pm. automation of the library is also going on.
